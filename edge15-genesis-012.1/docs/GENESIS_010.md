# Genesis-010 — Trade Review + Learning Log

Genesis-010 adds the first evidence loop to Edge15. The goal is to make it easy to record whether a signal worked, failed, was skipped, or was a good signal with a bad entry.

## Added

- Trade Review card after locking OVER or UNDER
- Outcome buttons: Won, Lost, Skipped, Bad Signal, Good Signal / Bad Entry
- Optional failure reason buttons
- Trade snapshots saved to local storage
- Recent Trades panel showing the last 10 Edge15 signals
- Daily/browser summary with reviewed trades, wins, losses, bad signals, bad entries, and win rate

## Saved at entry

- Direction
- Entry time/window
- Entry price
- Reference/strike
- Distance to reference
- Entry Score
- Opportunity
- Trade Grade
- Confidence
- Signal Stability
- Model Trust
- Settlement Risk
- Data source

## Notes

The journal is stored locally in the user's browser. It does not sync across devices yet.
