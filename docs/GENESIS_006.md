# Genesis-006 — AI Trading Desk v1

Genesis-006 adds the first Edge15 AI Trading Desk layer on top of the stable Genesis-005 trade-plan engine.

## Added

- Seven engine voices:
  - Trend Engine
  - Momentum Engine
  - Volatility Engine
  - Mean Reversion Engine
  - Market Pressure Engine
  - Kalshi Engine
  - Historical Pattern Engine placeholder
- Chief AI summary
- AI Debate panel
- Model Trust score
- Market Story powered by engine agreement/disagreement
- Why Not panel upgraded with engine blockers
- Hide/show workspace controls for major sections

## Preserved

- Genesis-002 live data engine
- Genesis-003 indicators and decision metrics
- Genesis-004 locked position mode
- Genesis-005 signal stability and trade plan

## Notes

The Historical Pattern Engine is intentionally a placeholder in Genesis-006. It uses the active signal plan and stability as a temporary fingerprint proxy until full historical fingerprint matching is wired in later.
