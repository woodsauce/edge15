# Edge15 Genesis-017 — Trade Quality + Auto-Tightening

Genesis-017 focuses on improving decision quality instead of adding clutter.

## Added

- Trade Quality score: AVOID / WEAK / DECENT / STRONG.
- Auto-tightening based on recent commitment performance.
- Late flip-risk detector for the final part of the 15-minute window.
- Trade replay snapshots for recent completed commitments.
- Extra entry gates for flip risk, auto-tightening, and trade quality.

## Goal

Edge15 should become more selective after weak performance. No Trade is preferred over forcing low-quality entries.
